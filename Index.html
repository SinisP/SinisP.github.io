Peter Sinis is an Economics major studying at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Peter is the oldest of five siblings and was born and raised in Harrison, New York. Growing up Peter had sensory issues which is a condition that affects how your brain processes sensory information. Sensory information includes things such as what you smell, see, hear, taste and touch. This was an issue that Peter struggled to deal with and had to see therapists growing up to fix this problem. While Peter outgrew these issues, he found that candles helped him cope with his problems. As Peter has grown older his hobbie of candles has grown. This website is for viewers to see Peters favorite candles that he has collected as well as scents and brands he does and does not reccomend. The companies that Peter will be reviewing are below.

Bed Bath and Beyond

Bed Bath and Beyond is an American chain of domestic merchandise retail stores. The chain operates many stores in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Puerto Rico. Bed Bath & Beyond was founded in 1971. They are known for selling products such as bath bombs, hand sanitizer, and candles.

Trueliving

True Living is a cheap inexpensive candleline created by Dollar General. They are in every Dollar General location with 17,915 stores in 46 states in the United States. The candles are made from soy wax in clear glass and commonly known as a low quality candle.

Yankee Candle

Yankee Candle is an American manufacturer and retailer of scented candles, candleholders, accessories, and dinnerware. Its products are sold by thousands of gift shops across the world, both instore, and online, and is in nearly 50 countries around the world.
